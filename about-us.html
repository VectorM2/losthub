Welcome to LostHub, a mobile application designed to help individuals and communities effectively manage lost and found items. Our mission is to reunite lost items with their rightful owners and make a positive impact on people's lives. We believe that by connecting lost and found items through our platform, we can build a stronger and more caring community.

LostHub serves as a centralized platform where users can report lost items they have found or search for items they have lost. With our user-friendly interface and extensive database, we increase the chances of successful reunions, bringing smiles back to those who have lost something dear to them.

Join LostHub today and be part of our mission to make a difference. Together, we can create a world where lost items find their way back home, and lost connections are rekindled.

Step-by-step proccess

  1. Step 1: Gather Information
    • Item Name
    • Item Description
    • Date when the item was lost
    • Contact Information (email/phone)
    • Optional: Image of the lost item
  2. Step 2: Access LostHub

    Open the LostHub app on your mobile device or visit our website.

  3. Step 3: Select "Report Lost Item"

    Click on the "Report Lost Item" button to begin the reporting process.

  4. Step 4: Enter Item Details

    Fill in the form with the information you gathered in Step 1.

  5. Step 5: Upload an Image

    If available, upload an image of the lost item to enhance the listing.

  6. Step 6: Submit the Report

    Review the information and click the "Submit" button to report the lost item.
